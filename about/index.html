About

Previously worked at LivingSocial and participated in Hungry Academy (aka Turing cohort 1203). Before programming, I studied Architecture, Arabic, and Near Eastern History in college. Currently devoting much of my time to getting better at Clojure and understanding cryptocurrencies through my Clarke Coin project.
